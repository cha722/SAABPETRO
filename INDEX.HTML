About Saab Petroleum

Founded in 1951, Saab Petroleum has been Lebanon's leading provider of premium fuels and automotive services for over 70 years. What began as a single service station in Beirut has grown into a network of over 180 locations across Lebanon.

Our commitment to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction has made us the preferred choice for generations of Lebanese drivers and businesses. We pride ourselves on our Lebanese heritage and our role in powering the nation's growth.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional energy solutions and automotive services that empower our customers and drive Lebanon forward.

Our Vision

To be Lebanon's most trusted energy partner, recognized for innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction.

Premium Oil Change Service

Our professional oil change service uses only the highest quality synthetic and conventional oils to ensure your engine runs smoothly and efficiently. We offer:

  • Full synthetic, synthetic blend, and conventional oil options
  • Oil filter replacement with premium filters
  • Multi-point vehicle inspection
  • Lubrication of chassis components
  • Fluid level top-off (windshield washer, coolant, etc.)

Regular oil changes are essential for maintaining engine health and preventing costly repairs. Our certified technicians will ensure your vehicle receives the proper oil type and viscosity recommended by your manufacturer.

Premium Fuel Technology

Our premium fuels are engineered with advanced additive technology to deliver superior performance, efficiency, and engine protection:
